Coraline Ismael Karim (she/her/hers) is an Indo-Iranian-Ecuadorian transgender woman born and raised in Tampa, Florida. She currently lives in Baltimore City, MD, and she has been published in University of Tampa's Talon Review, BULL Magazine, Exemplary Poetry, and has self-published several of her own works. Her writing ranges from literary fiction to gender horror to racism, transphobia, and violence turned into her own therapeutic art. You can find her biking through the city on her yellow-and-pink Surly Steamroller fixed gear bicycle with a tooth ratio of 46-20.

Soon a P.O. Box will be added here if anyone would like to contact her. She's a lover of handwritten letters!
