The Burden of the Shield
Behold the falling aegis by the crumbling hands of
Atlas, his hold burning from the seeping chaos
Of humanity. Their grubby fingers turn the Great Green Sphere
Malignant and rotted. Even the great titan can not withstand
The heavy weight of these growing and untamed parasites.
